# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in improving this operating model.

## What this repo is
A brand-free distillation of a methodology maintained privately and published as a marked snapshot.
This repo publishes the method; it does not track any private project's operational layer.

## How to contribute
- **Issues** — corrections, ambiguities, or "this didn't transfer to my context" reports are all
  welcome. Name the section and what broke; concrete beats abstract.
- **Pull requests** — fixes to wording, examples, or broken references are easy to take. Larger
  proposals (a new discipline, a changed rule) are best raised as a discussion first.

## How changes are decided
Method changes are evaluated and ratified by the maintainer before they land — the disciplines here
are deliberately not open to silent drift. A proposal that sharpens or clarifies has an easier path
than one that adds ceremony; the model is calibrated to demonstrated need, not to completeness.
